auto_stories Historical Significance

The **Sika Dwa Kofi** (Golden Stool) is the ultimate symbol of Ashanti unity and sovereignty. According to legend, it was summoned from the heavens by Okomfo Anokye and landed on the lap of Osei Tutu I, the first Asantehene. It contains the *Sunsum* (soul) of the Ashanti nation.

"Sika Dwa Kofi ne Asante Nation nkabom ne kyerɛwkyerɛw kɛseɛ. Wɔgye die sɛ ɛfiri soro na ɛbɛtoo Osei Tutu I nan so."
