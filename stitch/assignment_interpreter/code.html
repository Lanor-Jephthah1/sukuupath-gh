The Simple Gist

"Your professor wants you to look at how building new roads and bridges after Ghana became independent actually changed how people earn money and live their daily lives. You need to decide if these changes were mostly good or bad."

school

play_circle Key Action Words

search Evaluate compare_arrows Implications

Evaluate: Means to judge the value or quality of something. Don't just list facts; give your opinion based on evidence.

dictionary Important Terms

  • Post-colonial: The period after Ghana gained independence (after 1957).
  • Socio-economic: How social factors (people/culture) and economic factors (money/jobs) work together.

account_tree Suggested Answer Structure

1
Introduction (10%)

Define 'infrastructure' and briefly mention the time period you're discussing.

2
Economic Impacts (40%)

Discuss trade, jobs created by roads/ports, and cost of living changes.

3
Social Impacts (40%)

Discuss urban migration, access to schools/hospitals, and community connection.

4
Conclusion (10%)

Summarize if the overall result was positive or negative for Ghana.
